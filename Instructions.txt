You can not be connected to an account with Roblox and the bot at the same time. I recommend creating an account just to use the bot.

·Configure:

	1 - Edit 'bot.js'
	2 - Modify the fields between the "EDIT THIS" tags

·Turn it On - Windows:

	1 - Install Node.js https://nodejs.org
	2 - Shift+Right Click inside the folder of the Bot (.\RobloxManagerGroup)
	3 - Open PowerShell Window
	4 - Write: 'node bot.js'
	5 - Press Enter
	6 - Done
	
·Command:
	r!Promote <Roblox-User-ID> <Roblox-Role-ID>
